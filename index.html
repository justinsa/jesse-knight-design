Fire Protection Service

Jesse Knight Design is a Fire Protection Service company based out of North Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Our company has experience in all forms of construction, including single family dwellings, wood frame buildings, residential towers, commercial buildings, and industrial buildings. Our friendly staff has the knowledge and experience to accomplish your needs for fire protection.

Our work includes

  • Design drawings
  • Hydraulic calculations
  • Building schedules
  • Inspections
  • Prints - complete with Engineering and schedules
  • Customer service
  • Timely delivery

Additionally, we also provide Drafting services to create high-quality, professional drawings of an existing building.

We look forward to your business.

About Us

Our goal at Jesse Knight Design is to provide fully functioning designs at a minimal cost to our clients. We achieve our goal by creating efficient designs at competitive rates, with minimal pipe sizing that are proven to work through our hydraulic calculations. We also understand the importance of a deadline, and we are not afraid to work around the clock to make sure that your work is achieved when you need it to be.

We have a partnership with Cardinal Engineering Ltd. to provide their high-quality seal on our work.
